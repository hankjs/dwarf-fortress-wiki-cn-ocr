'''Wild carrots''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. The plant is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], (seeds when cooked), and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|carrot wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into pale brown [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] wild carrot plants for their ''edible roots''.


[[File:DaucusCarota.jpg|thumb|center|300px|Admired for its ''edible roots''.]]


{{D for Dwarf}}
Some dwarves kill themselves to obtain wild carrots, generally when a vile force of darkness arrives.
