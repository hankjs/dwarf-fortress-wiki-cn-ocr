'''Red beans''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons and only the seeds are edible when [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] red bean vines for their ''pods''.
