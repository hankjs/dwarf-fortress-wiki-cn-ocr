'''Brown bullheads''' are an aquatic [[vermin]]. They are found only in [[biome|temperate]] [[water|freshwater]] [[pool|lakes]], but can be seen year-round, and are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] brown bullheads for their ''whiskers''.
