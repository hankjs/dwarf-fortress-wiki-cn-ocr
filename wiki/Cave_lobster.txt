'''Cave lobsters''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] found only in [[caverns|underground bodies of water]]. Cave lobsters cannot be caught by [[fishing]], but instead must be caught in baited [[animal trap]]s. Once caught, [[fish cleaning|processing]] it will yield an [[food|edible cave lobster]] and a [[shell]] that can be used to craft or decorate items.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] cave lobsters for their ''beauty''.
