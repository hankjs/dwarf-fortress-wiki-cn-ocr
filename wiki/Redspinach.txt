'''Red spinach''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]] found in any biome except [[mountain]]s, [[glacier]]s, and [[tundra]]s. They are [[Herbalist#Gathering_plants|gathered]] and [[harvest]]ed as [[plant]]s which can be [[Thresher|processed]] at a [[Farmer's workshop]] to produce [[seed]]s and [[leaf|leaves]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into fuchsia [[dye]]. Seeds can be sown in all seasons, the leaves are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and seeds when cooked.

:''Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] red spinach for its leaves''.
