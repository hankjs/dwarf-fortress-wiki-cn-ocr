'''Muskmelon''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. The [[seed]]s can be planted in a [[farm plot]] in all seasons.  They grow into muskmelon vines, which can be [[harvest]]ed and [[process plants|processed]] at a [[farmer's workshop]] into muskmelons and more seeds.  The melon is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] muskmelon vines for their ''fruit''.
