'''No Exert''' creatures (those with the {{token|NOEXERT}} [[creature token|token]]) need no [[sleep]], and never become tired or exhausted. In the case of a particularly dangerous creature like a [[bronze colossus]], the NOEXERT tag guarantees that the foe will continue the battle as long as there are any [[dwarves]] left alive.
