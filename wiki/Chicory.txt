'''Chicory''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. It can be planted in all seasons and is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Wild ones flower, turning your landscape a pleasant blue hue.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] chicory for its ''taste''.
