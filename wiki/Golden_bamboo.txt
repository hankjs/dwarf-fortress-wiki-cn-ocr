'''Golden bamboo''' is a type of grass found in all dry broadleaf forests. It is one of the three types of grass upon which [[panda]]s can feed (the others being [[arrow bamboo]] and [[hedge bamboo]]).  Despite its name, it's the same color as other bamboos.
