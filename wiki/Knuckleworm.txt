'''Knuckle worms''' are a species of [[above ground]] [[vermin]] found in any [[evil]] areas that are not [[biome|freezing]], and may cause nearby [[food]] to [[rot]]. They are tied with the [[fox squirrel]] as the [[List of creatures by pet value|most valuable]] of all vermin. They can be captured by [[trapper]]s and turned into [[pet]]s.

Some [[dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] knuckle worms for their ''knobs and angles'' and their ''crackles and pops''.
