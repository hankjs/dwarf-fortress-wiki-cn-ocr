'''Sorghum''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]]. It is a type of grain and can either be [[Mill|milled]] into [[Flour|sorghum flour]], [[Still|brewed]] into [[Alcohol|sorghum beer]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into rust [[dye]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] sorghum for its ''beer''.
