'''Cabbage''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons and are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]]. Leaves can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into pink [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] cabbages for their ''taste''.
