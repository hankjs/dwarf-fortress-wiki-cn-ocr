'''Foxtail millet''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]]. It can either be [[milling|milled]] into [[flour|foxtail millet flour]] or [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|foxtail millet beer]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] foxtail millet plants for their ''grain''.
