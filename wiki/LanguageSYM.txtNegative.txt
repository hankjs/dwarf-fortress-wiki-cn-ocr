==Usage==
[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as a [[bogeyman]] or a [[nightmare]], associated with the [[sphere]] of misery will have words from this symbol in their name.

Alongside [[Language SYM.txt/Death|DEATH]], [[Language SYM.txt/Evil|EVIL]], and [[Language SYM.txt/Ugly|UGLY]], NEGATIVE is one of the possible [[Entity token#SUBSELECT_SYMBOL|subsymbols]] available to [[Surroundings#Evil|evil biomes]].

Most [[Entity|cultures]], except [[Goblin|goblins]], [[Entity token#CULL_SYMBOL|won't use]] words from this symbol in their names.
