'''Axolotls''' are a species of unremarkable [[vermin]] who can be found in [[tropical]] [[lake]]s. They may be captured by [[trapper]]s and turned into low-value [[pet]]s.

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] axolotls for their ''gills''.
