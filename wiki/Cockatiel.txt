'''Cockatiels''' are a species of [[above ground]] [[vermin]] who inhabit [[desert]]s and [[temperate]] [[grassland]]s. They may be captured in [[animal trap]]s and turned into [[pet]]s. All cockatiels possess Legendary [[skill]] in [[Climber|climbing]].

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] cockatiels for their ''crests''.
