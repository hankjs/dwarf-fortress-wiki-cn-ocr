'''Bambara groundnut''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons. Only the [[cooking|cooked]] seeds are edible.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] bambara groundnut plants for their ''edible nuts''.

===Bugs===

Although the seed material definition marks them as being edible raw, dwarves will not eat them.
