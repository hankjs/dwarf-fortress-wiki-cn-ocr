'''Blade weed''' is a [[plant]] used only to make emerald [[dye]] at a [[millstone|mill]] or [[quern]].  They can be planted outdoors any time of year and can be collected by [[Herbalist|gathering plants]] in any dry, [[Temperature|non-freezing]] [[biome]].

* Grow time: 300
* Plant value: 2
* Mill value: 20
* Dye [[color]]: Emerald
* Seasons: All

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] blade weed for their ''stiff, triangular leaves''.
