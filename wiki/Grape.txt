'''Grape''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden fruit]]. They can be planted in all seasons, the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into lavender [[dye]].

Grapes are a type of garden [[crop]] most known for being the "proper" source of wine.  Grape wine is merely called "wine", as opposed to other fruit wines.  

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] grape vines for their ''fruit''.
