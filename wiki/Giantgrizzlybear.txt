Stronger versions of the regular [[grizzly bear]], '''giant grizzly bears''' can be found in savage temperate biomes. They are capable of posing a threat to even a well-prepared fortress, and are best dealt with using [[cage trap]]s. Once trapped, they can be trained for hunting and war by an [[animal trainer]].
