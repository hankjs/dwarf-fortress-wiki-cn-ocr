'''Red squirrels''' are a type of [[above ground]] [[vermin]] found in any [[temperate]] [[forest]], but will not be seen during [[winter]]. They can be captured by [[trapper]]s and turned into [[pet]]s.

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] red squirrels for their ''tails''.
