'''Whip vines''' are an [[Tile attributes|above ground]] [[crop]] only found in [[Surroundings|savage areas]], and may be [[farming|planted]] at any time of the year, or [[Herbalist|gathered]] from outdoor [[shrub|shrubs]].  They cannot be eaten raw, but can be [[brew]]ed or [[mill]]ed. Whip wine, brewed from whip vines, is more [[value|valuable]] than any [[alcohol]] brewed from [[underground]] crops, and whip vine [[flour]] is the most valuable flour product available.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] whip vines for their ''length''.
