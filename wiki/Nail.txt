'''Nails''', '''claws''' and '''talons''' are a product of some [[animal]]s when [[butcher]]ed. They are stored in a [[stockpile]] that accepts [[body parts]], and [[refuse|have no known use]].

Civilized creatures may occasionally wear [[jewelry]] made out of nails, despite it being uncraftable in [[fortress mode]].

[[File:Cat_claw_closeup.jpg|thumb|270px|center|A cat claw nail and a human nail.]]

Should not be confused with hardware nails, which currently do not exist in the game. All wooden [[Building|buildings]] can be constructed with [[wood]] alone. These are fingernails or toenails. Thus, your [[Military|dwarves]] will only use their [[War hammer|hammers]] for pounding the latter type of nail. Hopefully not their own thumbs (ouch!).
