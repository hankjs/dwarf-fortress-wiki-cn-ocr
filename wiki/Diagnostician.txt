'''Diagnostician''' is the skill associated with the [[diagnosis]] [[labor]], as well as the hospital [[occupation]] associated with it. Diagnosers are responsible for deciding how a dwarf who is hurt should be treated at a [[hospital]].

Diagnostician [[skill|skill level]] is related to the speed at which the labor is completed, and it also affects the probability of identifying any active [[syndrome]]s. It is the only relevant skill of the [[chief medical dwarf]] noble position. Graphically, diagnosticians wear all black clothes.

Contrary to popular belief, an unskilled diagnoser will '''not''' misdiagnose injuries and prescribe inappropriate treatments.

Diagnosers determine the freshest hell unleashed on your fortress' injured as part of the [[health care|health care industry]].  The diagnostician skill is also used by [[scholars]] when thinking about medical science-related [[topics]]:
