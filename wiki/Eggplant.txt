'''Eggplants''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons and the seeds and fruit are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]]. Fruits also can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into lilac [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] eggplants for their ''fruit''.
