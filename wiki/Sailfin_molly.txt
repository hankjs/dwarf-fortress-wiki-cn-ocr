'''Sailfin mollies''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] found in and around [[temperate]] [[river]]s and [[lake]]s year-round, and are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] sailfin mollies for their ''wide dorsal fins''.
