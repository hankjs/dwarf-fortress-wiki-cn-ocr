'''Sandy clay loam''' is one of the many types of [[soil]] that can be found in [[soil layer]]s. Sandy clay loam layers occasionally contain an [[aquifer]].

Despite its name, it can neither be used as a source of [[sand]] for [[glassmaking]] nor [[clay]] for [[pottery]].
