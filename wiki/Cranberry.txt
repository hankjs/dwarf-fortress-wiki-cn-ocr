'''Cranberries''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|cranberry wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into red [[dye]].

They are hardy enough to endure even [[tundra]] environments.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] cranberry vines for their ''fruit''.
