'''Cave fish''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] only found in [[caverns|underground water sources]], but they can be seen year-round, and are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]]. Cave fish can be caught in any pool of water located within an appropriate cavern layer, whether or not it's connected to the map edge.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] cave fish for their ''beauty''.
