'''Lettuce''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They are [[Herbalist#Gathering_plants|gathered]] and [[harvest]]ed as [[plant]]s which can be [[Thresher|processed]] at a [[Farmer's workshop]] to produce [[seed]]s and [[leaf|leaves]], can grow wild or be planted in all seasons, and the leaves and plant are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] lettuce for its ''taste''.
