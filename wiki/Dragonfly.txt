'''Dragonflies''' are a type of [[above ground]] [[vermin]] found near [[pool]]s, and can be seen in any season other than winter. They may not be caught in built [[animal trap]]s. All dragonflies possess Legendary [[skill]] in [[Climber|climbing]]. In real life they have four wings, but in RAW has only two wings. 

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] dragonflies for their ''faceted eyes''.
