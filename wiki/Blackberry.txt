'''Blackberries''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden fruit]]. They can be planted in all seasons, the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|blackberry wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into lavender [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] blackberry bushes for their ''fruit''.
