'''Building materials''' are objects which can be used to build [[workshop]]s, [[construction]]s, and other types of [[building]]s. Most jobs which expect building materials also expect them to be non-[[economic]].

The following item types can be used as building materials:
* [[Stone]]
* [[Bar]]
* [[Block]]
* [[Log]]
