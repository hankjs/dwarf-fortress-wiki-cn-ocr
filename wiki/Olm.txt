'''Olms''' are a type of [[underground]] [[vermin]] commonly found in [[Caverns|subterranean]] [[lake]]s or [[river]]s. Unlike their larger, dangerous cousins, the [[giant olm]]s, they pose no threat to [[Dwarf|dwarves]].

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] olms for their ''gills''.
