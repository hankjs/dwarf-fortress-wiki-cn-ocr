'''Peach-faced lovebirds''' are a species of [[above ground]] [[vermin]], found in a variety of [[temperate]] [[biome]]s. They may be captured in [[animal trap]]s and turned into [[pet]]s. All peach-faced lovebirds possess Legendary [[skill]] in [[Climber|climbing]].

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] peach-faced lovebirds for their ''loving nature''.
