'''Beets''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|beetroot wine]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] beet plants for their ''edible roots''.
