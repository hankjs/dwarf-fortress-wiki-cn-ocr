'''Rhubarb''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons and is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]]. Leaves can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into tan [[dye]].

'''Don't try this at home!''' In real life, rhubarb leaves are toxic. The edible parts of the rhubarb are actually its ''petioles'', or leaf stalks.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] rhubarbs for their ''taste''.
