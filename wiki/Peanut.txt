'''Peanuts''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons and only the seeds are edible when [[cooking|cooked]]. Pods can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into dark brown [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] peanut plants for their ''edible nuts''.
