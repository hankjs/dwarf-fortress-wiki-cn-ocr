{{DFtext|Dwarf cancels Store Item: Job item misplaced.}}

This generally happens if, when an empty barrel or bin is assigned to a stockpile and the dwarves have started storing items in it, another dwarf comes along and picks up the container to haul it to another stockpile.

This error message is also given if you [[forbid]] the item that a dwarf was about to pick up.
