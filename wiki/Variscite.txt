'''Variscites''' are low-value green [[gem]]s found in [[bauxite]] clusters.

Since they occur in bauxite, along with [[ruby|rubies]] and [[sapphire]]s, they can occasionally overlap a cluster of these gems, which can prevent [[star ruby|star rubies]] and [[star sapphire]]s from appearing.
