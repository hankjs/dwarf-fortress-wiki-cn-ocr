'''Cave swallows''' are a species of unremarkable [[vermin]] which can be encountered in [[caverns]]. They can be captured by [[trapper]]s and turned into low-value [[pet]]s.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] cave swallows for their ''coloration''.
