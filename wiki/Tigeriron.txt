'''Tiger iron''' is an unremarkable low-value [[gem]] found in [[sedimentary layer]]s.

==In Real Life==
Tiger irons are related to the [[tigereye]] and [[cat's eye]] gems, where [[iron]] striations in the stone give the appearance of a tiger's stripes.

Despite their name, tiger irons cannot be used to make [[tiger]]s or [[iron]], nor have they been documented removing tigers from wheels.
