'''Elephant-head amaranth''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]]. They can be planted in all seasons, the leaves are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and the seeds when cooked.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] elephant-head amaranths for their ''leaf coloration''.
