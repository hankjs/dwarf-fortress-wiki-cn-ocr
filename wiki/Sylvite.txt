'''Sylvite''' is an unremarkable [[stone]] found in large quantities within [[rock salt]]. It is [[fire-safe]], but not [[magma safe]]. 

It is red-orange with [[premium]] graphics or yellow with [[classic]] graphics.

==In real life==
In real life, sylvite is potassium chloride in mineral form, making it a type of [[potash]], and is chiefly used for fertilizer.
