'''Fisher Berries''' are a type of [[above ground|aboveground]] [[crop]]. They can be found by [[Plant gathering|harvesting]] or by trading, or be [[Farming|grown]] on a surface [[farm plot]]. They can be [[food|consumed]] directly, or [[brewing|brewed]] into [[Alcohol|fisher berry wine]] and are stored in the 'Plants' section of food stockpiles.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] fisher berries for their ''round shape''.
