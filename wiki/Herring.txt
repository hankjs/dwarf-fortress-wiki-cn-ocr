'''Herrings''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] found in [[biome|arctic]] and [[temperate]] [[ocean]]s, and are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] herrings for their ''massive schools''.
