'''Leader''' is a miscellaneous [[skill]]. [[Main:Toady One|Toady One]] stated in a Fortress talk that it is used when a dwarf tries to teach another dwarf a skill through a lecture.{{verify}} It is currently only used to train [[weapon]] skills. All squad leaders ([[militia commander]], [[militia captain]]s, the [[captain of the guard]] and the [[hammerer]]) will learn it slowly.
