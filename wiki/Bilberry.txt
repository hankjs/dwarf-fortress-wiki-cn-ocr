'''Bilberries''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]] that highly resembles blueberries. They can be planted in all seasons, the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|bilberry wine]], or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into violet [[dye]].

When processed at a [[farmer's workshop]], bilberry bushes produce bilberries and seeds.  

They are among the [[blueberry|very]] [[cranberry|few]] crops that can be grown in a [[tundra]] environment.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] bilberry bushes for their ''fruit''.
