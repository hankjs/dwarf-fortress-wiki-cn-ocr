'''Arrow bamboo''' is a type of [[grass]] found in [[temperate]] [[Forest#Temperate_coniferous_forests|coniferous forest]]s. It is one of the three types of grass upon which [[panda]]s can feed (the others being [[golden bamboo]] and [[hedge bamboo]]).
