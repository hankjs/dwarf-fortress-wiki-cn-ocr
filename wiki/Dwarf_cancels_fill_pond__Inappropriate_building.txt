{{DFtext|Dwarf cancels fill pond: Inappropriate building}}

This error occurs if a [[dwarf]] tries to fill a [[pond]] and the pond zone isn't over [[open space]].
To fix it, [[channel]] underneath the pond zone, or delete the pond zone and make another over open space.
