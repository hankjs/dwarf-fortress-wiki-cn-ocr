==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as an amphibious [[forgotten beast]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of coasts, fish, fishing, lakes, oceans, rain, rivers, or water will have words from this symbol in their name. Those associated with lakes or oceans will also pull words from NAME_LAKE and NAME_OCEAN, respectively.
