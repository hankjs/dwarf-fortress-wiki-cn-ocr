The game interface is made up of several different parts. In the screenshot you will notice:

* '''A''': This is the area where [[announcements]] pop up.
* '''B''': In the lower left corner are menu buttons for [[unit list|creatures]], tasks, [[location|places]], [[labor]], [[work order]]s, [[noble]]s, objects, and [[justice]].
* '''C''': If enabled, the [[FPS]] counter is shown here, as game [[time|ticks]] (and graphical FPS).
* '''D''': The center [[designation]] tools shape the world:
** {{Menu icon|m}} [[Mining]]
** {{Menu icon|l}} [[Woodcutter|Logging]]
** {{Menu icon|g}} [[Plant gathering|Gathering]]
** {{Menu icon|v}} [[Smoothing]] and [[engraving|engra[v]ing]]
** {{Menu icon|x}} Removing [[designation]] orders
** {{Menu icon|b}} [[Building]]
** {{Menu icon|p}} [[Stockpile|Stock[p]iles]]
** {{Menu icon|z}} [[Zone]]s
** {{Menu icon|U}} [[Burrows|B[U]rrows]]
** {{Menu icon|h}} [[Minecart]] [h]aul routes ({{k|H}} could remain in saves created prior [[Release information/53.09#General updates|53.09]])
** {{Menu icon|T}} [[Traffic]]
** {{Menu icon|i}} [[Melt]], [[dump]], [[Hide Items or Buildings|hide]], or [[forbid]] [i]tems
* '''E''': The lower right corner are buttons for your [[military]] menu and an [[Civilization and World Info|overview]] of rest of the world.
* '''F''': This bar indicates how deep the currently viewed [[Z-level|elevation]] is; blue is above ground, the rest is below.
* '''G''': A mini-map of the current elevation your entire site with the area you can currently see outlined in yellow, and the specific elevation number below.
* '''H''': These are some game controls:
** Pause, play (can switch between these with {{k|Space}}), settings (also available in ESC menu), and help.
** [[Hotkeys|Hotkey menu]], also {{k|H}}.
** Move view to surface at this position.
** Move view to lowest revealed level at this position.
** Zoom view in, also {{k|[}} or {{k|Ctrl+Wheel}}, and display fluid level, also {{k|f}}.
** Zoom view out, also {{k|]}} or {{k|Ctrl+Wheel}}, and display ramp indicators, also {{k|r}}.
* '''I''': This is the overall [[Status]] of your fortress, including name, happiness levels, some food [[Stocks]] counts, and [[calendar]] information; this bar can vary in size and components depending on game resolution and UI scale settings.
