'''Satinspar''' is a type of [[gypsum]] that is fibrous. It is found within [[gypsum]] as [[vein|cluster]]s and can be used to make [[gypsum plaster]].

[[File:satinspar.jpg|thumb|250px|center|Also known as "desert rose", "gypsum flower" or "selenite".]]
