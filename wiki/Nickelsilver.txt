'''Nickel silver''' is a [[metal]] produced from a mixture of [[nickel]], [[zinc]] and [[copper]].  Despite its name, it does not contain [[silver]]. Because the components of nickel silver can be forged into (much more valuable) [[brass]] instead, there is no specific reason to create nickel silver unless one of your dwarves [[preferences|likes it]].

Smelting nickel silver produces 4 bars, meaning there is a net gain in value from 8 to 12.

Nickel silver is '''not''' magma-safe.
