'''Dimple cup''' is a [[underground|subterranean]] [[crop]]. It can be [[farming|grown]] year-round and can be [[milling|milled]] into [[dye|blue dimple dye]].
* Grow time: 500
* Plant value: 2
* Mill value: 20
* Dye color: Midnight Blue
* Seasons: All

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] dimple cups for their ''soothing color''.
