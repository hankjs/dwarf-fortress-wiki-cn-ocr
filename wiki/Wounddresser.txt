'''Wound dresser''' is the skill associated with the dressing wounds [[labor]] in [[healthcare]]. Wound dressers are responsible for cleaning wounds and applying bandages after they have been sutured, for which they need clean water, soap and [[cloth]] (any kind of cloth will do).

Wound dresser [[skill|skill level]] is related to the speed with which the labor is completed. Wound dressers try to stop the bleeding - oh Armok the bleeding - on your fortress' injured as part of the [[health care|health care industry]].
