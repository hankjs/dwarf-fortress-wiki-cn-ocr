==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]] associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of dawn, jewels, light, painting, or rainbows will have words from this symbol in their name.

Though not drawing from this symbol directly, some figures are named after the [[Descriptor color token#WORD|associated word]] to one of their [[Creature token#TL_COLOR_MODIFIER|described]] [[Color|colors]].
