'''Clear diamonds''' are particularly rare [[gem]]s, similar to [[black diamond|black]], [[yellow diamond|yellow]], [[red diamond|red]], [[blue diamond|blue]] and [[green diamond|green]] diamonds  - they occur only as individual gems within existing [[vein|clusters]] of [[faint yellow diamond]]s, which are themselves found only within [[kimberlite]], itself found only within [[gabbro]] [[stone layers|layers]]. Correspondingly, they are among the most [[value|valuable]] gems that can be found in ''Dwarf Fortress''.

{{Minorspoiler}}
Like all precious and rare gems, they may also make up part of the walls of [[unusual volcanic wall]]s.
