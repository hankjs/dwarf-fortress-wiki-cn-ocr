==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]] associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of consolation, healing, hospitality, peace, or silence will have words from this symbol in their name.

Alongside [[Language SYM.txt/Flowery|FLOWERY]], [[Language SYM.txt/Freedom|FREEDOM]], [[Language SYM.txt/Good|GOOD]], and [[Language SYM.txt/Truth|TRUTH]], PEACE is one of the possible [[Entity token#SUBSELECT_SYMBOL|subsymbols]] available to [[Surroundings#Good|good biomes]].

[[Goblin|Goblins]] will [[Entity token#CULL_SYMBOL|not use]] words from this symbol for their names.
