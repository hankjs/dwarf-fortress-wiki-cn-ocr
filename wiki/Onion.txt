'''Onions''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, and the bulb is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]]. Bulbs also can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into orange [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] onion plants for their ''bulbs''.
