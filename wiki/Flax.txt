'''Flax''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]] that may be processed into [[thread]] at the [[farmer's workshop]] or [[milling|milled]] into [[flour|flax flour]].

'''Flax seeds''' can be eaten raw, or milled into paste at a [[quern]] or [[millstone]], which in turn can be pressed into [[oil|linseed oil]] and a [[press cake]] at a [[screw press]].

Flax and [[hemp]] are the only millable plants that can be stored in containers made from their own fibers.

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] flax plants for their ''flowers''.
