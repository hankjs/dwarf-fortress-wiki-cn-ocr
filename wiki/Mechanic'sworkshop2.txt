The '''mechanic's workshop''' is a [[workshop]] which produces [[mechanism]]s out of [[stone]]*. It is also used to construct [[Health care#Traction benches|traction bench]]es.

: ''(* For mechanisms out of [[metal]], a mechanic must use a [[metalsmith's forge]] or [[magma forge]].)''

==Menu==
'''Mechanic's Workshop'''

*Make [[rock]] [[mechanism]]s
*Make [[Health care#Traction benches|traction bench]]

==Common Uses==
*Make [[Mechanism]]s for simple [[trap]]s, [[lever]]s, [[pressure plate]]s, and to link such triggers to [[door]]s, [[bridge]]s, [[floodgate]]s, [[cage]]s, more complex [[Trap_design|trap]]s, etc., etc., etc.
*Make [[Health care#Traction benches|Traction bench]]es to equip [[Activity_zone#Hospital|hospital]] [[zone]]s.

==Notes==
*[[Health care#Traction benches|Traction bench]]es require:
** a [[Rope]] (produced in [[Clothier's shop]]), or a [[Chain]] (produced in [[Metalsmith's forge]])
** a [[Table]]
** a [[Mechanism]]
* For non-dwarf fortresses, the name of this workshop will change to match if your fortress's primary race provides a custom value for the {{token|PROFESSION_NAME|c|MECHANIC}} token.
