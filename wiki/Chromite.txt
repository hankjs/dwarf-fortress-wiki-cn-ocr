'''Chromite''' is a dark purple stone found within [[olivine]] as veins, and notable in that it is the sole source of valuable [[demantoid]] gemstones, as well as being a potential source of [[native platinum]]. Like olivine, chromite is also [[magma-safe]].

==In real life==
In real life, chromite is the sole ore of chromium.
