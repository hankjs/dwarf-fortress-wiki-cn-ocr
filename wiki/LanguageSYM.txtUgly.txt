==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as a [[hydra]] or [[minotaur]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of blight, deformity, depravity, disease, muck, or nightmares will have words from this symbol in their name.

Alongside [[Language SYM.txt/Death|DEATH]], [[Language SYM.txt/Evil|EVIL]], and [[Language SYM.txt/Negative|NEGATIVE]], UGLY is one of the possible [[Entity token#SUBSELECT_SYMBOL|subsymbols]] available to [[Surroundings#Evil|evil biomes]].

Most [[Entity|cultures]], except [[Goblin|goblins]], [[Entity token#CULL_SYMBOL|don't use]] words from this symbol for their names.
