==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as a [[bronze colossus]] or a [[minotaur]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of chaos, nightmares, revenge, valor, or war will have words from this symbol in their name.

Most [[Entity|cultures]] use this as [[Entity token#SUBSELECT_SYMBOL|subsymbol]] for describing [[war]] and other historical conflicts.
