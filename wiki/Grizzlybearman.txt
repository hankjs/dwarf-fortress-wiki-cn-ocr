'''Grizzly bear men''' are [[animal people]] variants of the common [[grizzly bear]] who can be found in [[savage]] [[temperate]] [[forest]]s and [[taiga]]s. They spawn in groups of 2-10 individuals and can be a threat to a fortress, as they are over twice the size of a [[dwarf]] and can easily maul one to pieces with their teeth and claws.

Like other savage animal people, they can join [[civilization]]s, become [[historical figure]]s, appear as [[visitor]]s, and be playable in [[adventurer mode]].

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] grizzly bear men for their ''strength''.
