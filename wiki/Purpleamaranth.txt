'''Purple amaranth''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]] that can be [[milling|milled]] into [[flour|purple amaranth flour]] or brewed into purple amaranth beer. Its leaves are a second product that is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] purple amaranths for their ''striking color''.
