'''Potatoes''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. Potato seeds can be planted in all seasons.  Potato plants are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|potato wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into beige [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] potato plants for their ''edible tubers''.


[[File:Patates.jpg|thumb|center|300px|Admired for its ''edible tubers''.]]


{{D for Dwarf}}

One can succeed in making an entire dwarven civilization [[fat|obese]] by starting a chain of [[kitchen]]s that serve +potato biscuits+ made with minced potatoes and finely minced cow tallow, and call it McDwarf's.
