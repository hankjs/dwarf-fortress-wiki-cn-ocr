'''Peddler''' is a profession for some inhabitants of generated [[site]]s, like [[town]]s. Some peddlers show up as [[visitor]]s in fortress mode, and [[agent]]s sometimes try to pose as peddlers. Peddlers are [[Unit_type_token#PEDDLER|shown in grey]] ({{Dwarf|7:0}}). 

==Fortress mode==
Peddlers will not [[immigration|migrate]] to your fortress in this version.
