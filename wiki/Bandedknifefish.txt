'''Banded knifefish''' are aquatic [[vermin]] that can be found in [[biome|tropical]] [[water|freshwater]] [[river|rivers]] and [[pool|lakes]] year-round, and are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] banded knifefish for their ''rippling fin''.
