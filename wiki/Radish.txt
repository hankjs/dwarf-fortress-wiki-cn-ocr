'''Radishes''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|radish wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into pale pink [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] radish plants for their ''edible roots''.
