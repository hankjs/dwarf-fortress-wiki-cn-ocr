51.13 was a hotfix released on June 27, 2025, one day after [[Release information/51.12|51.12]], on the [https://www.bay12forums.com/smf/index.php?topic=183326.0 Bay12 Forums] and [https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/975370?emclan=103582791464215078&emgid=547864982714319413 Steam].

== General updates ==
* Fixed a timing error introduced by ammo loading that caused many actions to be performed in slow motion, leading to all of the dwarves dying over a long period as drinking could not catch up with thirst.
* Adventurer can wait when under attack while nocking or loading.
