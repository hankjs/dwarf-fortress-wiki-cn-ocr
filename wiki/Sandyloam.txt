'''Sandy loam''' is one of the many types of [[soil]] that can be found in [[soil layer]]s. Sandy loam layers occasionally contain an [[aquifer]].  It is not to be confused with [[loamy sand]].

Despite its name, it can ''not'' be used as a source of [[sand]] for [[glassmaking]].
