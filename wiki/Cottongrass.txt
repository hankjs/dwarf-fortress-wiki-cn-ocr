'''Cottongrass''' is a type of grass that grows exclusively in otherwise-inhospitable [[tundra]] and [[mountain]]ous regions. During late spring it produces a growth, white cottongrass tufts.  Unlike [[cotton]], it cannot be used to produce [[thread]].
