The '''County arc''' is one of the [[Development arc|arcs of development]] of Dwarf Fortress.

==Dwarf Fortress Consolidated Development==
While outdated, Toady has explained what to expect from this arc:
<blockquote>
You should be able to develop a larger settlement outside the confines of the immediate play area.
</blockquote>
This arc may have been completed by the site [[holding]] feature.
