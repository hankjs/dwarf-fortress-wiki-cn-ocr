==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as a [[bronze colossus]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of earth, jewels, metals, minerals, or mountains will have words from this symbol in their name. Those associated with mountains will also use NAME_MOUNTAIN to generate names.

[[Dwarf|Dwarves]] particularly [[Entity token#SELECT_SYMBOL|value]] this symbol (alongside [[Language SYM.txt/Artifice|ARTIFICE]]) and will include [[Dwarven language|words]] from it to name citizens, sites and governments.
