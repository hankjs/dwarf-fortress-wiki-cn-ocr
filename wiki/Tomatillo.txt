'''Tomatillos''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], or seeds when cooked, and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|tomatillo wine]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] tomatillo plants for their ''fruit''.


[[File:Tomatillo.jpg|thumb|center|300px|Admired for its ''fruit''.]]


==In Real Life==
Tomatillos are also known as Mexican [[husk]] tomatoes.
