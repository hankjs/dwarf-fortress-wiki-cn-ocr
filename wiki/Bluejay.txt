'''Blue jays''' are a type of [[above ground]] [[vermin]] found in any [[temperate]] area with [[tree]]s. They may be captured in [[animal trap]]s and turned into [[pet]]s. All blue jays possess Legendary [[skill]] in [[Climber|climbing]].

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] blue jays for their ''coloration''.
