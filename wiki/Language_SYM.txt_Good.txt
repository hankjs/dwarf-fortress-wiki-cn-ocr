==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]] associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of charity, consolation, forgiveness, generosity, happiness, healing, hospitality, justice, loyalty, mercy, revelry, or truth will have words from this symbol in their name. It is unknown if the [[Creature token#GOOD|GOOD]] token also has this linkage.

A total of twelve spheres all use this symbol.

Alongside [[Language SYM.txt/Flowery|FLOWERY]], [[Language SYM.txt/Freedom|FREEDOM]], [[Language SYM.txt/Peace|PEACE]], and [[Language SYM.txt/Truth|TRUTH]], GOOD is one of the possible [[Entity token#SUBSELECT_SYMBOL|subsymbols]] available to [[Surroundings#Good|good biomes]].

[[Goblin|Goblins]] will [[Entity token#CULL_SYMBOL|not use]] words from this symbol for their names.
