'''Steelhead trout''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] found in nearly all [[temperate]] [[river]]s and [[ocean]]s year-round. They are also found in [[biome|arctic]] oceans. Steelhead trout are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] steelhead trout for their ''coloration''.
