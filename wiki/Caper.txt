'''Capers''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons and the berries and leaves are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] caper bushes for their ''buds and berries''.
