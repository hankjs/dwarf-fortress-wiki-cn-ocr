'''Watermelons''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden fruit]]. They can be planted in all seasons and the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]]. Fruits also can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into orchid pink [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] watermelon vines for their ''fruit''.
