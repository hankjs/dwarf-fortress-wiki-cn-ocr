'''Pendant amaranth''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop]]. It can either be [[milling|milled]] into [[flour|pendant amaranth flour]] or [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|pendant amaranth beer]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] pendant amaranths for their ''striking color''.
