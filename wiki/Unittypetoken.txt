=== Profession Categories ===
Layered graphics only use profession categories rather than individual professions. These are:

* {{Dwarf|7:1|17px}} STONEWORKER
* {{Dwarf|7:0|17px}} MINER
* {{Dwarf|0:1|17px}} METALSMITH
* {{Dwarf|4:1|17px}} ENGINEER
* {{Dwarf|6:0|17px}} FARMER
* {{Dwarf|6:1|17px}} WOODWORKER
* {{Dwarf|2:1|17px}} JEWELER
* {{Dwarf|2:0|17px}} RANGER
* {{Dwarf|3:0|17px}} STANDARD (i.e. "Peasant". Also includes some invader & visitor professions.)
* {{Dwarf|1:1|17px}} CRAFTSMAN
* {{Dwarf|1:0|17px}} FISHERY_WORKER
* {{Dwarf|7:0|17px}} MERCHANT
* {{Dwarf|4:0|17px}} CHILD
* {{Dwarf|5:0|17px}} NONE (applies to anything not included above - doctors, bards, military, etc. Note that this is distinct from having no profession condition; it will apply ONLY to NONE units.)

Noble positions do not affect the categorization; a craftsdwarf appointed to mayor will still use the layers designated for <code>CRAFTSMAN</code>.
