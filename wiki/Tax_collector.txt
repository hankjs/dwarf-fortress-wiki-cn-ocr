The '''Tax collector''' does not exist in this version of Dwarf Fortress.

[[40d:Tax collector|Before it was removed from the game]], the tax collector was a [[noble]] who collected taxes from your dwarves on behalf of the [[baron]], as part of the [[dwarven economy]].

The tax collector was removed, along with the rest of the dwarven economy, in DF v0.31.01. [[Toady One]] has stated that the dwarven economy may return after sweeping changes are made, although such changes are [http://www.bay12forums.com/smf/index.php?topic=169696.msg8451817#msg8451817 "a long way off"].
