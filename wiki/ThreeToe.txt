'''ThreeToe''' is the nickname used by '''Zach Adams''', co-designer of ''[[Dwarf Fortress]]'' and author of the [http://www.bay12games.com/dwarves/dev_story.html short stories] which highlight ''Dwarf Fortress''<nowiki>'</nowiki>s upcoming features. He can be reached at [http://www.bay12games.com/about.html About Bay 12 Games] or the [http://www.bay12forums.com/smf/ Bay 12 Games Forum].

The other designer, and programmer, is Zach's younger brother, Tarn ([[Toady One]]).

ThreeToe married on July 15, 2017. All we know about his spouse Annie Forsman-Adams is that she has "royal blood". According to him, their wedding was planned to be "as dwarfy as possible." [http://www.bay12games.com/dwarves/dev_2017.html#2017-07-13]

Since March 2019[http://www.bay12games.com/dwarves/dev_2019.html#2019-03-09], ThreeToe has been keeping four smaller [[cavy|threetoes]]: [[Harry, Ramone, Sid and Bowie]].
