'''Giant grey parrots''' are 507 times larger than [[grey parrot]]s, and, like most giant creatures, produce more [[food]].

Some dwarves like giant grey parrots for their ''intelligence'' and their ''social nature''.  
==Technical==
The [[/raw|raw]] name for these creatures is, rather logically, <code>GIANT_GREY_PARROT</code>, compared to <code>PARROT_GREY</code>, the name of [[grey parrot]]s.
