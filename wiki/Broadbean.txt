'''Broad beans''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons. Only the seeds when [[cooking|cooked]] are edible.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] broad bean plants for their ''pods''.
