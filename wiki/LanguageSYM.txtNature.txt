==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as a [[roc]] or a [[force]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of agriculture, animals, fish, fishing, hunting, nature, plants, seasons, or trees will have words from this symbol in their name.

[[Elf|Elves]] particularly [[Entity token#SELECT_SYMBOL|value]] this symbol (as well as [[Language SYM.txt/Flowery|FLOWERY]]) and will include [[Elven language|words]] from it to name citizens, sites and governments.
