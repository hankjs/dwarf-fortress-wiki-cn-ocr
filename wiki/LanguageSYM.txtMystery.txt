==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as many [[Forgotten beast|forgotten beasts]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of darkness, dreams, dusk, fate, lies, mist, the moon, nightmares, rumors, the stars, theft, treachery, trickery, and twilight will have words from this symbol in their name.

A total of fourteen spheres use this symbol.
