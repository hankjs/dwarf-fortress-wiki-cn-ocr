'''Hake''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] found in [[temperate]] and [[biome|arctic]] [[ocean|oceans]] year-round, usually in large clusters. They are a ready source of [[food]] when caught and [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] hake for their ''notched fins''.
