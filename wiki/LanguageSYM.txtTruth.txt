==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]] associated with the [[Sphere|sphere]] of truth will have words from this symbol in their name.

Alongside [[Language SYM.txt/Flowery|FLOWERY]], [[Language SYM.txt/Freedom|FREEDOM]], [[Language SYM.txt/Good|GOOD]], and [[Language SYM.txt/Peace|PEACE]], TRUTH is one of the possible [[Entity token#SUBSELECT_SYMBOL|subsymbols]] available to [[Surroundings#Good|good biomes]].
