'''Bubble bulbs''' are special plants found only in [[Surroundings#Good|good]]-aligned regions, providing nourishment for the majestic [[unicorn]]s and other fine creatures that happen to live there.

As they are a type of [[grass]], [[Pasture#List_of_grazing_animals|grazing animals]] can feed on bubble bulbs inside their assigned [[pasture]].
