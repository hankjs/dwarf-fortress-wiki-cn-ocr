'''Red-winged blackbirds''' are a type of [[above ground]] [[vermin]] found flying around any [[temperate]] [[marsh]] year-round. They may be captured in [[animal trap]]s and turned into [[pet]]s. All red-winged blackbirds possess Legendary [[skill]] in [[Climber|climbing]].

Some dwarves [[Preferences|like]] red-winged blackbirds for their ''coloration''.
