'''Apple''' is one of the many genera of [[tree]]s found above ground. Like most trees, apple [[wood]] is brown and produces brown products.

Apple trees bear apples that can be harvested by setting up a gathering [[zone]]. They can be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into delicious [[alcohol|apple cider]], so you may consider exploiting them, rather than chopping them down to make [[charcoal]]. They also drop leaves, which can be collected in bags. Apple leaves are not cookable or brewable, though they can be made into [[dye]].

Logs and leaves can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into [[dye|dyes]] - logs for rose and leaves for yellow.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] apple trees for their ''fruit''.
