'''Chickpeas''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons and the seeds are only edible when [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] chickpea plants for their ''pods''.
