A [[creature]]'s {{token|MULTIPLY_VALUE|md}} affects the [[item value|value]] of animal products such as [[meat]], raw hides, [[silk]] [[thread]], and anything made from these raw [[material]]s, including [[leather]] or silk [[clothing]]. While it's possible for {{token|MULTIPLY_VALUE|md}} to apply to only specific parts of a creature, all non-modded creatures use ``SELECT_MATERIAL:ALL``, so all [[Body parts|parts]] of the animal are multiplied.
