'''Turnips''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|turnip wine]] or processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into beige [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] turnip plants for their ''edible roots''.
