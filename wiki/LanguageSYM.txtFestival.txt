==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]] associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of festivals or revelry will have words from this symbol in their name. 

The symbol used for naming festivals celebrated in [[Legends mode]] is a separate token, NAME_FESTIVAL.
