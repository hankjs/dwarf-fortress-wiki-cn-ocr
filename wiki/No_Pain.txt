[[Creature]]s with the '''{{token|NOPAIN}}''' [[creature token#N|creature token]] feel no [[Symptom#Pain|pain]] in (or out of) [[combat]]. These creatures will suffer grievous [[wound]]s and continue fighting without pause, making them significantly harder to kill - many foes are dispatched by a blow to the head after they fall [[unconscious]] from pain. Luckily, creatures with this token are rare.
