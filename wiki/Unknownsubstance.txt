'''Unknown substance''' is a [[hardcoded material]] that takes the form of a light gray liquid. It freezes at [[temperature|{{ct|10000}}]] and boils at [[temperature|{{ct|10180}}]], and has the same density as [[water]]. It was well-known in v0.28.181.40d for appearing in glowing pits occupied by [[40d:tentacle demon|tentacle demon]]s, along with both types of [[filth]].
