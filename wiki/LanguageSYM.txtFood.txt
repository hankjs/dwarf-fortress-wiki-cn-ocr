==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]], such as a [[giant]], associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of agriculture or food will have words from this symbol in their name.

A noun from [SYMBOL:FOOD] is used as the [[Entity token#SELECT_SYMBOL|symbol]] for naming a [[tavern]], in the form "The [ADJECTIVE] [FOOD]" or "The [FOOD] of [NOUN]". The other words are defined by what symbols the [[entity]] has assigned to the [[Entity token#SELECT_SYMBOL|OTHER]] context (often through REMAINING or ALL).
