[[File:bay12_logo.png|thumb|150px|right|Company logo]][http://www.bay12games.com/ Bay 12 Games] is a game company owned by [[Toady One|Tarn Adams]] and his brother [[ThreeToe|Zach Adams]]. It currently develops and publishes ''[[Dwarf Fortress]]'', and has also created several ASCII games.

Its motto is ''"Beyond Quality."''

[[Kitfox Games]] is responsible for contracting the new additions to the team for art, audio, and programming since the [[Premium]] update. ([https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/975370/view/4237410439220288178?l=english Summer 2024 FAQ])   They are listed, among others, in /data/credits.txt.
