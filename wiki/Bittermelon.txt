'''Bitter melons''', also known as goya, are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons, and grow leaves and a melon. The melon can be edible after being [[cooking|cooked]], but the leaves are edible raw or cooked.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] bitter melon vines for their ''taste''.
