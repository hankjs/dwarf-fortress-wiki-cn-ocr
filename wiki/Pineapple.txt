'''Pineapples''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|pineapple wine]]. Leaves can be processed in a [[dyer's shop]] into golden yellow [[dye]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] pineapple plants for their ''fruit''.

[[File:കൈതച്ചക്ക.jpg|thumb|center|300px|Admired for its ''fruit''.]]

{{D for Dwarf}}

Unfortunately, dwarven scientists have so far failed entirely to make houses out of pineapple - building them under the sea to house a [[sponge man]] is out of the question.
