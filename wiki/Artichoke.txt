'''Artichokes''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons and grow a purple flower, leaves, and an artichoke heart. The artichoke heart is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|artichoke wine]].  [[Seed]]s can only retrieved from brewing, not from eating raw hearts.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] artichokes for their ''yummy hearts''.
