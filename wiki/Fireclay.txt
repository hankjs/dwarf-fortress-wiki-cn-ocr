'''Fire clay''' is one of the many types of [[soil]] that can be found in [[soil layer]]s. [[Aquifer]]s are never present in fire clay.

Fire clay is a special variety of [[clay]] that is used in the production of [[stoneware]], which needs no glazing to store liquids.
