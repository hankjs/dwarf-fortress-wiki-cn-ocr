'''Rock salt''' is a [[sedimentary]] [[stone]]. It can contain [[vein|clusters]] of [[sylvite]] and [[borax]].

{{D for Dwarf}}

Rock salt can also form during [[tantrum]]s, especially after [[losing]].
Dwarven kids use rock salt as a [[pacifier]]; it's a salty start to a heavy life!


==In real life==
Rock salt, known officially as halite, is the mineral form of sodium chloride (NaCl). It is found as isometric crystals of a colorless or white appearance. Large deposits of it are found near [[lake]]s and [[Ocean|seas]].
