'''Squash''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]] (though botanically a fruit). They can be planted in all seasons and its fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] squash vines for their ''fruit''.
