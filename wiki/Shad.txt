'''Shad''' are a type of aquatic [[vermin]] found in nearly all [[temperate]] and [[biome|arctic]] [[river]]s and [[ocean]]s year-round. They are a ready source of [[food]] when [[fish cleaning|cleaned]] at a [[fishery]].

Some [[dwarves]] [[Preferences|like]] shads for their ''silvery bodies''.
