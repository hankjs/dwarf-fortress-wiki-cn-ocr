'''Winter melon''' is an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons and the fruit is edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]].

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] winter melon vines for their ''fruit''.


[[File:W tougan4091.jpg|thumb|center|300px|Admired for its ''fruit''.]]

{{D for Dwarf}}

Winter melons are especially beloved by dwarves, also being called [[tallow]] gourds.

Winter melons are prized for [[defense guide|fortress defense]] because of their ability to deal heavy damage and slow groups of [[undead|zombies]].
