'''Parsnips''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden vegetable]]. They can be planted in all seasons, can be eaten raw or [[cooking|cooked]], can be brewed into [[alcohol|parsnip wine]], and the seeds are only edible when cooked.

In real world it is a root vegetable closely related to the carrot.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] parsnip plants for their ''edible roots''.
