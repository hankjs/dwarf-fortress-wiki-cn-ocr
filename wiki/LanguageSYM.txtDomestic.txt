==Usage==

[[Historical figure|Legendary beings]] associated with the [[Sphere|spheres]] of family, festivals, hospitality, or labor will have words from this symbol in their name.

Most [[Entity|cultures]], except [[Human|humans]], [[Entity token#CULL_SYMBOL|don't use]] words from this symbol for their names.

Ribald [[Performer#Poet|poetic forms]] use this symbol in place of the typical [[Language SYM.txt/Flowery|FLOWERY]].

A noun from [SYMBOL:FOOD] is expected to be used as the [[Entity token#SELECT_SYMBOL|symbol]] for naming a [[market]], in the form "The [ADJECTIVE] [DOMESTIC]" or "The [DOMESTIC] of [NOUN]". The other words are defined by what symbols the [[entity]] has assigned to the [[Entity token#SELECT_SYMBOL|OTHER]] context (often through REMAINING or ALL).
