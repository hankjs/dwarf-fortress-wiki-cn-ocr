'''Passion fruits''' are an [[aboveground]] [[crop|garden plant]]. They can be planted in all seasons, are edible raw or [[cooking|cooked]], and can also be [[brewing|brewed]] into [[alcohol|passion fruit wine]].

Passion Fruits are stockpiled in the 'Fruit/Leaves' section in food stockpiles. In the stocks menu, it's listed under 'Leaves'.

Some [[Dwarf|dwarves]] [[preferences|like]] passion fruit vines for their ''fruit''.
